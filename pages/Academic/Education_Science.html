Overall Program Aim

The overall aim of the program is to develop students’ capacity in using English in all genres of competencies regionally and internationally. The graduates will have an in-depth understanding of all kinds of social affairs communicated in English within a wide range of political economy. Four macro-skills are polished in order to broaden language proficiency, fluency, and accuracy.

Program Mission

The program is ready to commence with quality and standard in accordance to National Qualifications Framework for Higher Education in 2015. Therefore, the university's mission is to nurture academic excellence as the country’s premier business-knowledge-enhanced place of learning, providing business know-how for graduates in all disciplines, and to stay ahead in shaping business leaders and knowledge experts of tomorrow in an international academic and research atmosphere.

In order to achieve the above goals, the program’s curriculum development is a must. It focuses on fostering critical and creative thinking and independent working skills so that students enter the professional world more fully equipped to respond to its needs with knowledge in both English language and business.

Quality and Staff Development

Quality concern is another portion that the university never takes for granted; after having a good curriculum, staff training focus is also crucial in order to maximize the English education quality. Staff members need to be constantly provided with in-service training—staffs who have been employed receive more training to advance their instruction ability.

Role of Department of Higher Education

In Cambodia, the Department of Higher Education (DHE) plays an important role in overseeing and maintaining the implementation of the curriculum. This institution conducts workshops and other meetings with specialists and relevant stakeholders to develop and cross-check the quality and implementation of the curriculum. The curriculum must meet the academic requirements of assisting students to learn and develop cognitively in an optimum way, while equipping students with the professional skills and knowledge necessary for success in highly competitive national and international environments.

English in Business

English is the global language and is increasingly necessary in all areas of business, especially with the coming 2015 ASEAN Community where English will definitely become the means of communication. Like all nations, countries in the region are not immune to world economic trends, and have been affected by recent global economic crises.

For Bachelor Degree graduates, the job market is as competitive as ever, and this competition has been added to by a growth in the number of higher education institutions. Curricula must equip students with as many skills and competencies as possible if these students are to succeed in this very competitive environment.

1. Associate Degree

Vision

The Associate Degree Program in English is designed for highly qualified associate diploma holders to meet workforce demand where English is tremendously used. Learners will acquire practical English language skills that can be applied effectively in various fields such as teaching, tourism, business, and financial sectors, among others. Moreover, this program develops personal confidence and competency in communication skills that require English. It provides not only the hard skills related to English use but also soft skills that enable learners to integrate into the business world and culture.

Goal

Our program aims to produce human resources that meet the current trend of the job market, where English is widely used. It also aims to equip the successful degree holders from this program to fit themselves into the regional competitive working environment, especially with the upcoming ASEAN community integration by 2015.

Mission

Our mission is to provide concrete and useful knowledge for learners in a very short time through an applicable mechanism:

  • Enriching curriculum knowledge and practice to prepare learners for the real work environment.
  • Effective instructions for student activity orientation.
  • Highly qualified lecturers—both academically and pedagogically.
  • Sufficient time and content to ensure learners are effectively transmitted knowledge.
  • Cooperation with partnered companies to provide internship opportunities and prospective employment.
  • Exchange programs with partnered universities abroad to regionalize and globalize learners' knowledge.

3. Bachelor Degree

The Bachelor program in English is designed to meet workforce demand where English is tremendously used. The graduates from this program are expected to work in the fields of teaching, translating and interpreting, as well as those in tourism, hospitality, and business, particularly where English proficiency is required. In response to the need for capable human resources, Phnom Penh International University (PPIU), with its Faculty of Education Science, has established three English programs:

  • English for Education: Focuses on Teaching English as a Foreign Language (TEFL).
  • English for Business: Aims to provide English for work skills, offering vocationally focused courses for administrative and developmental roles.
  • English for Specific Academic Purposes (ESAP): A distinct program promoting English among students in other academic fields, provided to students who take business-related fields from Year I to Year II semester I.

All students must complete the university's Foundation Year program, acquiring general knowledge and ethical principles necessary for further study. For students in the English for Education and English for Business programs, they will take the same courses throughout Years II and III before specializing in their area of English studies in Year IV.

The English for Education program develops students' professional skills in TEFL at secondary and tertiary school levels. A practicum (teaching practice) and ongoing individual learning are key aspects of the course.

The English for Business program enhances students' English knowledge and ability for employment in various administrative and developmental roles requiring high-level English skills. In Year IV, students specialize in subjects like Business Communications, Public Relations, International Business, Research Methodology, Translation Skills, and English for Business.

Admission

In order to enroll in the program, all students must:

  • Hold a High School Certificate or a qualification deemed equivalent by the Ministry of Education, Youth and Sport (MoEYS). Alternatively, a B.A. or an associate degree in any field from a recognized university or institute.
  • Pass a 90-minute English entrance examination administered by the Faculty of Education Science, PPIU.
  • Fill out the formal application form, which is essential for registration and validation by the Ministry of Education, Youth and Sports.

Assessment and Examination

Students' academic performance is assessed seriously each semester. A good score in one semester cannot compensate for a fair or poor score in another. Assessment is carried out in two ways:

  • Ongoing Assessment (55%) based on:
    • Class attendance and participation (10%)
    • Homework, assignments, oral presentations (20%)
    • Mid-term test (25%)
  • Final Examination (45%)

The ongoing assessment score is announced at the end of the course before the final exam. Students will know how many marks or scores they have earned and how many more they need to achieve.

Before announcing the final result, the management team and academic personnel cross-check the accuracy and validity of the results to ensure fairness.

Promotion

Students must fulfill all academic and attendance requirements to be promoted to the next level. In order to pass a subject, students must achieve a score of at least 60%.
